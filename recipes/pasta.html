PASTA

Description

Pasta is also an Italian Delicacy.Pasta comes in too many Shapes and Sizes and Each and Every one of those has a Different name for itself.

Just like pizza Cooking Pasta takes Patience and a Little more work than your Average Recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (ANY TYPE)
  • Olive Oil
  • Pasta Sauce or Pasta Tastemaker
  • Tomatoes
  • White Onions
  • (Optional) White Pasta Sauce
  • (Optional) Red Pasta Sauce
  • Cilantro
  • Garnishing (YOUR CHOICE)

STEPS

  1. Take the pasta Put it in a Boiling Pot and Set it to Boil at Medium Flame.
  2. Take cilantro and chop it Finely.Cut some onions in rings and Some Tomatoes.
  3. Once the Pasta is Boiled, Take a strainer and Put the pasta in the Strainer.Now use clean water to Once again Strain the pasta with.
  4. Heat a Pan put in Olive oil once the oil starts to heat put in the all the vegetables and toss the pan continously for 20 Seconds then leave it for a Minute or Two (Depends on how cooked you want the veggies to be) now put the Pasta in the Pan and Reduce the flame or If you're using Induction reduce the Power to 40 Percent.
  5. (OPTIONAL) Add a Pasta Sauce of your choice
  6. Turn off the heat, cover it with a Lid and Let sit for 3 Minutes
  7. Serve the pasta with garnishing of Your choice.ENJOY

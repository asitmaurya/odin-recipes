PASTA

Description

Pasta is also an Italian Delicacy.Pasta comes in too many Shapes and Sizes and Each and Every one of those has a Different name for itself.

Just like pizza Cooking Pasta takes Patience and a Little more work than your Average Recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (ANY TYPE)
  • Pasta Sauce or Pasta Tastemaker
  • Tomatoes
  • White Onions
  • (Optional) White Pasta Sauce
  • (Optional) Red Pasta Sauce
  • Cilantro
  • Garnishing (YOUR CHOICE)

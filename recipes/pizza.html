PIZZA

DESCRIPTION

Pizza is an Italian recipe and the most famous snack after the Hamburger.It's not just easy to eat but also Really simple to make if you want to bake it Yourself.

It has simple ingredients and most of the baking and cooking is done wile you are Sitting waiting for the time to pass.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Dry Oregano
  • Few Olives
  • 2 Cups of Dough
  • Dry Yeast
  • Pizza Seasoning
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Olive Oil

STEPS

  1. Put some water in a Pot and Put it to Boil.Take the Tomatoes, peel them and wait for the Water to Boil.Add a pinch of salt to the Water once it starts to Boil and then Add the Tomatoes.
  2. Take the dough put it in a Doughmaker and 1 Tsp of Yeast to it and add some water and start the Doughmaker and wait for the Dough to get ready.While you are getting the dough ready Take the Tomatoes out and Use a blender to make Tomato Puree.
  3. Put a pan on Stove add some Olive oil to it and then add the Tomato Puree.Add Salt according to Taste and then add 1 Tsp of Oregano.Keep Stirring for a minute then turn the Flame off.
  4. Once the Dough is Ready put it aside for 2 Hours.Cover the Tomato Puree for the time being.
  5. After 2 Hours, Take the Dough and make Pizza Base from the Dough using a Rolling pin or By hand.Now use a brush to Lighly oil the Pizza Base with Olive Oil.
  6. Put the Pizza base on a Clean Flat Surface add the Tomate Puree and Spread it evenly using a Ladle.Now take the Mozarella and Grate it over the Pizza Evenly until you can berly see the Tomato Puree on the Pizza.
  7. Preheat the Oven at 220 Degrees.Put the Pizza in and Set the timer to 8 mins.Now wait for the Pizza to cook.
  8. Once the Timer Beeps take the Pizza Out Season it with the Pizza Seasoning use a Pizza Cutter or a Knife to cut the Pizza Even Slices.
  9. Serve the Pizza with Ketchup or Hot Sauce.
